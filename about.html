Pop Culture Info Nuggets

  • The game was popularized by the TV show "The Big Bang Theory"
  • There, genius theoretical Physicist Sheldon Cooper introduces this version of the game to show off his to his friends
  • Originally, Blogger Sam Kass came up with the variation of the game together with Karen Bryla, with the former claiming that the show producer didn't ask permission to use it in The Big Bang Theory
  • The show rectified this behind-the-scenes resulting in Sheldon giving Kass the credit he deserved for it in season 5, episode 17 titled "The Rothman Disintegration."
