The Rules

This game is an extended version of Rock Paper Scissors.

Choose between rock, paper, scissors, lizard and spock.

Once you have, these are all possible outcomes of the pairings:

Scissors cuts Paper, Paper covers Rock, Rock crushes Lizard, Lizard poisons Spock, Spock smashes Scissors, Scissors decapitates Lizard, Lizard eats Paper, Paper disproves Spock, Spock vaporizes Rocks and finally, Rock crushes Scissors.

Go and see if you can beat the computer!
